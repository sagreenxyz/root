Welcome to your encrypted content vault!

This is a demo note that is automatically re-encrypted at build time using
your site's configured credentials (AUTH_HASH). It will always be decryptable
by any user who logs in successfully.

You can add your own private notes by running:

  node scripts/encrypt-content.mjs <username> <password> your-file.txt \
      src/data/encrypted/your-file.json

Or place plaintext files in src/data/plaintext/ to have them encrypted
automatically during every build.
